The EverAfter Journey

Our Meticulous Pathway to Your Vows

Click each planning milestone to explore how we curate and safeguard your big day.

Month 1 - 3

Curating Your Vision Board

We begin by understanding your individual stories, design inspirations, and budget priorities. We build a personalized mood board defining your custom floral styles, lighting direction, and overall aesthetic template.

  • 3D Virtual Venue Scouting
  • Preliminary Budget Modeling
  • Guest List Allocation Guidelines
Month 4 - 6

Securing Master Venues & Vendors

We leverage our extensive international relationships to vet, negotiate, and book the absolute top caterers, florists, and production crews matching your wedding vision and financial priorities.

  • VIP Vendor Contract Negotiations
  • Guest Room Block Organization
  • Entertainment & Lighting Production
Month 7 - 9

Refining the Aesthetics

We hold menu tastings, construct customized mockup tablescapes, and map out detailed spatial layouts for your reception, ceremony, and cocktail hours to ensure absolute visual harmony.

  • Fine Floral & Place Card Curation
  • Custom Stationery & Invitation Suite
  • Detailed Master Production Timeline
